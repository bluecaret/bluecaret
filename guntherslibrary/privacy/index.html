Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This app is designed to respect your privacy by collecting no personal data.

1. Offline Functionality

The app works entirely offline. It does not require or use an internet connection for its core features.

2. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No device identifiers

3. No Accounts

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in. Everything is stored locally on your device.

4. No Ads

This app contains no advertising of any kind.

5. In-App Purchases

You may choose to unlock Premium features via a one-time in-app purchase.

  • Purchases are processed securely by the Google Play Store.
  • We do not have access to your payment information.

6. Local Storage Only

All data is saved on your device and never transmitted to any server. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted unless you have backed it up.

7. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data is collected at all, it is safe for all ages.

8. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We do not collect or process personal data, so the app is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Users are not tracked, profiled, or subject to any data collection.

9. Changes to This Policy

If there are any changes to this policy, they will be reflected in an updated version of the app and noted here.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, you may contact us at: dev@bluecaret.com
